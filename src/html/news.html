Today I have been setting up and configuring DKIM, SPF, and DMARC.
Self-hosting email is highly discouraged, but I really want to dig in deep into the requirements and keep up to date with how everything works.
Most organizations simply offload the heavy-lifting to Microsoft, Google, or even Yahoo.
This is recommended and completely fine.
